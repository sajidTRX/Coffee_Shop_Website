History

Nestled in the heart of Rivea, our coffee shop began with a passion for bringing people together over exceptional brews. Since 1800, we’ve served handcrafted coffee and locally sourced treats in a cozy atmosphere. From the first sip to the last, our mission is to create a welcoming space where community and flavor come alive.

About our Customers and their reviews

Rivia, the birthplace of Geralt of Rivia, is a small yet significant kingdom nestled within the Northern Realms. Known for its rugged landscapes and industrious people, it is a land rich in history and tradition. Despite its modest size, Rivia has endured through countless conflicts, its people embodying resilience and strength. Geralt’s title as “of Rivia” ties him to this region, although his Witcher origins hail from Kaer Morhen, a testament to the duality of his identity.

Geralt

Yennefer of Vengerberg is a powerful and enigmatic sorceress, known for her striking beauty, sharp wit, and immense magical prowess. Born with a hunchback and facing a difficult childhood, she transformed herself through magic to become one of the most influential mages in the Continent. Despite her stern exterior, Yennefer has a deep capacity for love, particularly for Geralt and their adoptive daughter, Ciri. Her strength and determination make her a pivotal figure in shaping the world's fate.

Yennefer

Cirilla Fiona Elen Riannon, known as Ciri, is the Lion Cub of Cintra and the adoptive daughter of Geralt and Yennefer. A princess by birth, she is also the last of the Elder Blood, inheriting extraordinary powers tied to ancient elven magic and time-space manipulation. Ciri’s destiny is intertwined with the fate of the Continent, making her a target for many who seek to exploit her abilities. Brave, resilient, and compassionate, she grows from a lost child into a fierce warrior determined to control her own fate.
